Which figure types should FrameForge support?

Select all that make sense for photography poster designs. Your current shape layer already has line and rectangle (with border radius). These are the proposed additions:

A

Circle / Ellipse

Badges, circular frames, highlight rings, oval overlays. Very common in poster design.

B

Triangle

Directional pointers, geometric decorations, corner accents, dividers.

C

Arrow

Callouts, directional indicators, "pointing to" elements in the photo.

D

Diagonal / Angled Line

Separator lines at any angle — adds dynamism to layouts. Extend the existing line shape with rotation/angle support.

E

Regular Polygon (Hexagon, Pentagon, Star)

Geometric accent shapes. Hexagon, pentagon, diamond, star. Trendy in modern poster design.
